Subscription Rules

For Lenders

  • Must have an active subscription to lend.
  • Subscription expires on the 28th of each month.
  • Expired subscriptions block all lending features.
  • Choose one tier at a time; upgrade/downgrade available.
  • Payment redirects to M-Pesewa Till (simulated).
  • No cross-group or cross-country lending.

For Borrowers

  • Borrowers pay no subscription fees (except higher tiers for larger loans).
  • Can join up to 4 groups with good rating.
  • Loan requests are free; only interest applies.
  • Blacklisted borrowers cannot borrow or join new groups.
  • All loans are off-platform (M-Pesa, bank, etc.).
